Staff Who Keep Properties Running

A well-managed property depends on the right people showing up, doing the job, and doing it consistently. We source, screen, and place skilled staff across every function a property manager needs to fill.

Whether you manage a single residential complex or a portfolio of commercial properties, we match you with candidates who understand the environment and can hit the ground running.

Roles We Fill

Property Staffing Across Every Function

From day-to-day housekeeping to technical maintenance and tenant-facing roles, we cover the full scope.

Housekeeping

Reliable housekeepers for residential complexes, sectional title schemes, and short-term rental properties. Daily, weekly, and contract arrangements available.

Maintenance & Operations

General maintenance workers and handymen who handle day-to-day upkeep - plumbing, electrical, painting, and general repairs across residential and commercial sites.

Tenant Relations

People-facing staff who handle tenant communication, complaints, move-in and move-out coordination, and general admin. Professional, calm, and organised.
