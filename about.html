Our Story

Established in 2026. Built on Trust.

Mywash Recruitment Agency is a wholly Black-owned and managed South African company with a practical understanding of people, placement, and service delivery.

From our base in Gauteng, we provide temporary and permanent recruitment support across Johannesburg, the wider Gauteng region, and other South African provinces.

We serve both sides of the employment relationship with equal commitment. Whether you are looking for your next great hire or your next great role, we treat your outcome as our responsibility.

What Guides Us

Our Mission in Practice

We use our understanding of the South African labour market to create placements that support candidates, clients, and long-term business goals.

We listen before we act

Every brief, every CV, and every conversation gets our attention before we make a recommendation. We take the time to understand the requirement clearly.

We work with discipline

Our process is structured, responsive, and focused on the outcome. Clients and candidates know what to expect at each stage.

We think long term

A placement should support more than an immediate vacancy. We match for fit, reliability, and the environment the person will be joining.

We serve both sides equally

Employers and candidates both deserve a great experience. We hold ourselves to the same standard on either side of the table.
