Our Story

A New Agency With Deep Roots

Mywash Recruitment Agency was founded in Gauteng with a single conviction: that the right person in the right role changes everything. For the individual, it means purpose. For the organisation, it means performance.

We offer temporary and permanent recruitment across Johannesburg and the wider Gauteng region, bringing clients the candidate networks, location knowledge, and hands-on expertise to make every placement count.

We serve both sides of the employment relationship with equal commitment. Whether you are looking for your next great hire or your next great role, we treat your outcome as our responsibility.

What Guides Us

Our Values in Practice

These are not words on a wall. They are the behaviours you will see from us every time we work together.

We listen before we act

Every brief, every CV, every conversation gets our full attention before we make a single recommendation. We take the time to get it right.

We keep our word

If we commit to a timeline, a shortlist, or a follow-up, we deliver on it. No chasing. No excuses.

We think long term

A placement that does not last is not a success. We match for fit, culture, and growth potential, not just for the immediate requirement.

We serve both sides equally

Employers and candidates both deserve a great experience. We hold ourselves to the same standard on either side of the table.
